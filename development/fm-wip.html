Web Animation for FileMaker's WIP page

2019

View on Website

While contracting at FileMaker, I designed and coded an animated portion of one of the main web pages for FileMaker's marketing website. The images fading in and the lines connecting the images being drawn are animated in CSS. The animations are triggered based on scroll position using jQuery. I was initially animating the three main images in code, but the team decided gifs would be more reliable, so I made those animations in After Effects.
